# ♻️ Recycling Exchange Marketplace  
**G18 Hackathon – January Edition**  
**Problem Statement ID:** 18  
**Track:** Circular Economy & Waste Management  

---

## 🌍 Mission

Build a **B2B marketplace** that connects **businesses** and **recyclers** to **buy, sell, or trade recyclable scrap materials**.

Large volumes of recyclable materials—**plastics, metals, paper, and glass**—end up in landfills due to poor coordination between scrap generators and recyclers. The current recycling supply chain is **fragmented, opaque, and inefficient**.

This platform aims to:
- Streamline scrap material transactions  
- Improve access to recyclable resources  
- Reduce landfill waste  
- Support and strengthen the **circular economy**

---

## 📈 Impact Scope

- Designed for **national-scale deployment**

- Helps achieve **waste reduction targets**
